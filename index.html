Dirichlet calibration: a well-founded and easily implemented improvement on logistic calibration for multiclass classifiers

Meelis Kull, Telmo de Menezes e Silva Filho and Peter Flach

Dirichlet calibration is a multi-class generalisation of Beta calibration. We are in the process of writing a paper on Dirichlet calibration.

If your k-class classifier is producing probability estimates p1,...,pk, then Dirichlet calibration is equivalent to logistic regression in the logarithmic scale with k features ln(p1),...,.ln(pk).
